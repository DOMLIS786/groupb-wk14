Methods

For the usability test all participants were sent a link to the Group B website and a link to the anonymous Google survey. After navigating through site participants were asked to complete the survey. Eight of the subjects were also asked if they had any additional feedback about the website, specifically the navigation, design, and content. They were provided a list of three open ended questions. This portion was optional.

Demographics

Participants were asked three demographic questions at the beginning of the survey:

Question 1: Were you familiar with 3D printing prior to taking our test?

Results:

  • 66.7% of our participants answered "No"
  • 33.3% of our participants answered "Yes"

Question 2: What is your age bracket?

  • 46.7% of our participants selected 30-39 years-old
  • 26.7% of our participants selected 20-29 years-old
  • 13.3% of our participants selected 40-49 years-old
  • 6.65% of our participants selected 13-19 years-old
  • 6.65% of our participants selected 50+ years-old

Question 3: How often do you use computers or websites?

  • 93.3% of our participants selected "Always"
  • 6.7% of our participants selected "Frequently"

Results

Home Page Question

100% of participants found the homepage easy to navigate

Tutorial Page Question 1

93% of particpants found the content to be clear.

Tutorial Page Question 2

80% of particpants reported no compatibility issues.

FAQ Page Question

80% of participants reported the page provided explaination about 3D printing in the Rebecca Crown Library.

Faculty Page Question

Only 53% of participants reported to be interested in 3D printing as coursework.
